About

Hi!

I'm Ben, a generalist games programmer in the second year of my degree at the University of the West of England, studying Games Technology BSc (Hons). I'm currently on placement at Playground Games.

I'm experienced in programming with a variety of tools and languages, notably C++, C#, DirectX, OpenGL, Unity and Unreal, and in both an object oriented and data driven fashion utilising an Entity Component System.

Alongside my studies I have created a number of tools and applications, including a full release game: Wardens Teddy - a 2D platformer where you have to work back through the prison you once patrolled to rescue your lost friend, all while dodging traps and solving puzzles meant for escapees!

Since secondary school I have been interested in computer science and creating games, the discipline allowing for both creativity and problem solving with the result being something that can actually give others a meaningful experience. Some of my favorite games include Subnautica, The Forest and Terraria. I love multiplayer survival games that give you the freedom to do what you like, whilst also having a challenging but attainable end goal.

Outside of programming I enjoy running, swimming, reading and of course playing games! Recently I have begun learning Korean and have so far reached an intermediate level.
