About

Hi!

I'm Ben, a 19 year old Game Developer currently in my second year towards my degree in BSc (Hons) Games Technology at the University of the West of England.

I have created a number of games and applications using various tools and languages, with my most recent project being Wardens Teddy - a 2D platformer where you have to work back through the prison you once patrolled to rescue your lost friend, all while dodging traps and solving the puzzles meant for the escapees!

Since secondary school I have been interested in computer science and creating games, the discipline allowing for both creativity and problem solving with the result being something that can actually give others a meaningful experience. Some of my favorite games include Subnautica, The Forest and Terraria. I love multiplayer survival games that give you the freedom to do what you like, while also having an attainable end goal.

Outside of programming and playing games, I enjoy reading, exercise and the outdoors. Recently I have begun learning Korean and have so far reached an intermediate level.
